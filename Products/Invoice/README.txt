International

   We made this product to cover our own need. It complies with all neccesary standards for Norwegian invoices. We'd like it to be usable for as many countries as possible, but not to have special condition for every contry in the world. Feel free to add *generic* fields and modifications that make the invoice usable as many places as possible, but not locking it to your standard.
